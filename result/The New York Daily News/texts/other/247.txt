An e-bike rider died after slamming into a parked SUV on a Brooklyn street Monday morning, police said.
A man operating an electric moped smashed into the rear of a parked Toyota Four Runner SUV in front of 622 Greene Ave. in Brooklyn on Monday. (Theodore Parisienne/for New York Daily News)
Luciano Morales Feliciano, 25, was riding a 2021 Fly-9 electric scooter about 4:46 a.m., going east on Greene Ave. near Tompkins Ave. in Bedford-Stuyvesant, when he lost control.
A man on an electric moped smashed into a parked SUV in front of 622 Greene Ave. in Brooklyn on Monday. (Theodore Parisienne/for New York Daily News)
He crashed into a parked 2004 Toyota 4Runner, police said.
Morales Feliciano lived in Bushwick about two miles from the crash. Medics took him to Kings County Hospital, but he couldn’t be saved.
