LABRADOR RETRIEVER | Labrador retrievers are consistently the most popular dog breed in the United States, and for good reason. The breed is friendly, outgoing, trainable and plays well with kids. The breed is also endlessly entertaining with its versatile set of skills, which include hunting, diving and tracking. (Radomir Rezny/Shutterstock)
 When adding a pup to your family, it can be difficult to decide which breed is right for you. To find the best dog breeds, The Active Times consulted the American Kennel Club on what breeds it recommends for families. (Zareen Syed, The Active Times)
