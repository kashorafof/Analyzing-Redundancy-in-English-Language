AUSTIN, Texas—Pete Arredondo, the school-district police chief at the center of the broadly criticized response to last month’s school massacre in Uvalde, Texas, has been put on administrative leave effective Wednesday, according to the district.
Uvalde Consolidated Independent School District Superintendent Hal Harrell said in a written statement that though he had pledged to wait until the investigation was concluded to make any personnel decisions, he opted to put Mr. Arrendondo on leave now “because of the lack of clarity that remains and the unknown timing of when I will receive the results of the investigations.”
