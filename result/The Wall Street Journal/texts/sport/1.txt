What makes for a great golf course?
To get the answer to that question, we turned to Gil Hanse, who over the past two decades has catapulted into the elite of golf-course architects. Hanse Golf Course Design, where he works with design partner Jim Wagner, has been called on by some of the game’s most prestigious clubs to restore or renovate their courses.
