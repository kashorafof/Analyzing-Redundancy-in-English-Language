Idaho police arrested 31 people believed to be part of the white nationalist group Patriot Front near an annual LGBT Pride event on Saturday. 
Police had been on high alert after being warned that a number of groups planned to interrupt the annual Pride in the Park event in Coeur d’Alene, Idaho.
