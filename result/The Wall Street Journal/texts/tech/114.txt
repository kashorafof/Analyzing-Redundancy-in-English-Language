MSNBC has named veteran political correspondent Alex Wagner to succeed Rachel Maddow, the network’s highly rated anchor, as the host of its 9 p.m. hour.
Ms. Wagner, 44, is set to begin hosting her show starting on Aug. 16, MSNBC said in a statement. She previously hosted an MSNBC show, “Now with Alex Wagner,” from 2011 to 2015, and she occasionally filled in for Ms. Maddow in recent months.
