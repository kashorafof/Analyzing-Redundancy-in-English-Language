John Bergler, 80, a retired co-owner of a company that manufactured tools for automotive production who lives in suburban Las Vegas, on his 1959 Cadillac Coupe de Ville, as told to A.J. Baime.
In 1989, while I was still working and living outside of Detroit, my wife, Lorraine, and I came to Vegas on vacation. We used to do this a couple times a year, and I would structure our trips around an automotive auction or convention, because I love cars and my wife liked casinos more than I did. So, this particular trip was planned around a car auction.
