Tony Siragusa, a former defensive tackle for the Baltimore Ravens and Indianapolis Colts, has died. He was 55 years old.
“The Goose squeezed 200 fun loving years into 55!! He was one of the most physically strongest players I have ever seen in 50 years,” Colts owner Jim Irsay said in a statement on Twitter Wednesday, referring to Mr. Siragusa by his nickname.
