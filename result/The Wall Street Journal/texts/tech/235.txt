What sport can offer so fine an opportunity as golf to play with—or compete against—old friends and create new ones?
In fact, playing golf enjoyably may well depend more on one’s choice of golfing companions than on one’s score—and never more so than in one’s latter years, when there’s more time for swinging on the links. Doubly fortunate is the player whose partners and opponents provide good company off as well as on the course, especially at the 19th hole.
