Sky-high ticket prices, snaking airport lines and flight cancellations and delays aren’t the only stressors passengers face as air travel approaches prepandemic levels.
Annoying in-flight behaviors from other passengers are back and multiplying as planes fill up. Bare feet on the seats or bulkhead. Watching movies without headphones. Throttling the seat in front of you on the way into or out of your row.
