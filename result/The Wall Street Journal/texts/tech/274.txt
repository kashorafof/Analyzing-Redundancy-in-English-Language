HIGHLAND PARK, Ill.—Police apprehended a 22-year-old man as a suspect in a shooting at a July Fourth parade in this Chicago suburb that left at least six people dead and more than two dozen wounded.
Robert E. Crimo III from Highland Park was caught late Monday in a car in the nearby town of Lake Forest following a brief pursuit, after he was spotted by an officer, according to local law enforcement.
