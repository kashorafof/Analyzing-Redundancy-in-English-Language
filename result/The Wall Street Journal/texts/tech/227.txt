An anonymous bidder has bid a record $19 million to have the opportunity to eat lunch with Warren Buffett.
The individual, who made the winning bid in a charitable auction on eBay, will meet the Berkshire Hathaway chief executive at New York City steakhouse Smith & Wollensky sometime in the coming months.
