Today’s tweeds turn away from tradition by combining the coolness of Clueless’s Cher Horowitz and the chicness of Coco Chanel. The tasteful, transitional textile has long been a top choice for designers looking to weave some personality and pizzazz into their outerwear and beyond.
A punchy, pink Chanel coat, shown here on model Tess Carter, gets a dressed-down twist with a relaxed pair of B Sides blue jeans. Turn heads in tranquil turquoise from Proenza Schouler’s White label or make your wardrobe sing with Oscar de la Renta’s canary-yellow houndstooth mini set.
