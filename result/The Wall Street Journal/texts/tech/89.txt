When Scottie Scheffler holed out for eagle on the 8th hole to take a two-shot lead at this U.S. Open, it looked like the world’s No. 1 golfer and recent Masters champion might just take control of this major. But amid brutally difficult conditions at the Country Club, nobody has held on to the lead for long. 
The result entering the final round on Sunday is a quickly shifting and crowded leaderboard—and it features many of the game’s youngest emerging superstars.
