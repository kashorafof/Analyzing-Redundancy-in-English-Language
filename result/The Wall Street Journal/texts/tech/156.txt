Ryan Kelley, a Republican candidate for Michigan governor, was arrested by federal agents at his home on Thursday morning in connection with his role in the Jan. 6, 2021, attack on the U.S. Capitol, authorities said.
The Federal Bureau of Investigation arrested Mr. Kelley, 40 years old, on four charges connected to the Capitol riot, according to the agency and the U.S. attorney’s office for the District of Columbia.
