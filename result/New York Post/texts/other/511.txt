A man was shot and critically wounded walking across a Queens street in broad daylight on the Fourth of July, police and police sources said.
The bullets flew at 208th Street and 116th Avenue in Cambria Heights around 3:10 p.m., leaving the victim, in his 30s, with a gunshot wound to his hip, police said. 
The man, who wasn’t immediately identified, was taken to Long Island Jewish Hospital in critical but stable condition, cops said. 
Four or five gunshots were fired before the victim was found in the middle of the street, police sources said. It wasn’t immediately clear if he was the intended target. 
The gunman fled the scene in an unknown direction.
