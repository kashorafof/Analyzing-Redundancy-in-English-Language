A 60-year-old man was struggling to survive after he was struck by a car in a hit-and-run crash in the Bronx Sunday morning, cops said.
The man was crossing in front of 1101 Westchester Avenue in Foxhurst around 3:40 a.m. when he was hit by a sedan that was traveling west, cops said.
He suffered trauma to his body and was taken to Lincoln Hospital in critical condition. The sedan’s driver fled the scene, cops said.
