Paul Mudrick, an investment banker focused mainly on healthcare who lives near Philadelphia, and his daughter Lisa Cavanaugh, a software industry executive living in the Boston area, on a 1977 Porsche 930 Turbo, as told to A.J. Baime.
Mr. Mudrick: In December 1976, I went to the Porsche dealer near where I lived in Pennsylvania. I loved cars so much, I used to go there just to hang out. I had ordered a new silver 911. But while I was there, I saw this car on a lift in the service area. I went right into the office of the dealership owner and asked if I could buy this other car and if he could sell the car I had ordered to someone else. He said sure. That’s how I came to buy this 1977 model year 930 Turbo [also known as the 911 Turbo].
