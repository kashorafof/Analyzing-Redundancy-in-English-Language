Four sportswear brands are teaming up to help recycling technology get to market more quickly as the clothing industry comes under pressure to reduce waste.
French startup Carbios SA said Wednesday it signed a two-year agreement with Patagonia Inc., Puma SE, On Holding AG and Salomon SAS to hasten the commercialization of its biorecycling technology, which uses enzymes to break down polyester for reuse in new products.
