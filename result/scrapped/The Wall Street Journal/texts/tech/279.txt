Ukrainian troops repelled a Russian attack on one of Ukraine’s last lines of defense to protect the remaining Kyiv-held strongholds in eastern Ukraine, while the head of Russia’s security council accused the North Atlantic Treaty Organization of stoking further confrontation between Russia and the West.
Russian troops have advanced toward the Donbas cities of Sloviansk and Kramatorsk, the last main cities in the region still under Ukrainian control following months of slow Russian gains in the region.
