WIMBLEDON, England— Novak Djokovic’s chase for all-time greatness is back on. After a full year of tumult, uncertainty and no major titles, he is once again a Grand Slam champion.
Facing Nick Kyrgios, one of the most volcanic tempers on the professional tour, Djokovic delivered a composed, clinical performance to win 4-6, 6-3, 6-4, 7-6(3) and claim his fourth straight Wimbledon title and seventh overall. The victory, his 21st at a Slam, put Djokovic just one major behind Rafael Nadal and one ahead of Roger Federer on the all-time list.
