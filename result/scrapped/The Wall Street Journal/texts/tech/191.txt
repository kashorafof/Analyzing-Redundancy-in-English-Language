Crowded airport parking lots are fueling stress for travelers. 
Travelers are packing their bags in greater numbers, and more fliers are driving to the airport than before the Covid-19 pandemic, parking managers and airport directors say. And a growing percentage of those who park at or near airports are making reservations in advance, leaving even fewer spaces for day-of travelers.
