Golfers may hope to get on a hot streak, but a new study suggests that isn’t likely. In fact, it’s more likely they will find themselves stuck in a cold streak.
Using data from 119 PGA Tour and LPGA Tour tournaments, the researchers found no evidence golfers even have hot streaks, which they define as success building on itself for successive holes. When the researchers looked at birdies, eagles or better, they didn’t find that the score predicted better performance on the next hole.
