It takes a lot to shake confidence in Reliance Industries India’s most valuable company. An unprecedented windfall tax on fuel exports, which pushed the stock down by as much as 9% on Friday, seemed to do the trick.
But even a big new tax isn’t enough to end bumper profits at the firm’s energy wing—thanks to cheap Russian oil.
