With just weeks to go before opening, many summer camps still haven’t hired enough workers to staff all of their programs.
The average cost to hire camp staff this summer has increased at least 10% to 15%, according to Tom Rosenberg, chief executive of American Camp Association, an industry group. Some camps are offering signing bonuses, recruiting from new places like local senior centers, and offering hires more flexible schedules.
