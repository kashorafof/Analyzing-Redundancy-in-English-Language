More than 5,000 flights were canceled by U.S. carriers over the busy Father’s Day and Juneteenth holiday weekend despite industrywide efforts to tackle the knock-on effects of staff shortages and inclement weather.
Almost a third of flights arriving into U.S. airports were delayed on Friday and 6% were canceled on Thursday as the problems almost matched those over the Memorial Day holiday.
