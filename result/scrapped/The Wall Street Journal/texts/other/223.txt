The Biden administration has abandoned former President Donald Trump’s proposal to alter Air Force One’s longstanding color scheme by painting the fleet of presidential aircraft red, white and blue.
A Biden administration official said Mr. Trump’s proposal isn’t being considered because it would require additional engineering, as well as increased time and cost. Officials are considering several other paint schemes but haven’t yet made a final decision. The configurations include versions of the current paint scheme, according to a person familiar with the matter.
