Hearings by the House select committee investigating the Jan. 6, 2021, storming of the Capitol by a mob of supporters of Donald Trump pick up again Monday morning.
In her opening testimony Thursday night, Rep. Liz Cheney (R., Wyo.) provided a road map to the final five hearings as the committee makes its case—that former President Trump was accountable for the attack and that it was the result of a multipronged conspiracy—to the American public.
