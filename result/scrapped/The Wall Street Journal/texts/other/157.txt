CONWAY, S.C.—Rep. Tom Rice said he voted to impeach Donald Trump to hold the former president accountable for his actions when a mob of his supporters broke into the Capitol on Jan. 6, 2021.
That vote could cost the five-term congressman his seat in this conservative coastal district when he faces a Trump-endorsed challenger, state Rep. Russell Fry, in the primary Tuesday. Mr. Rice stands by his impeachment vote and said constituents should look at the rest of his record in Congress.
