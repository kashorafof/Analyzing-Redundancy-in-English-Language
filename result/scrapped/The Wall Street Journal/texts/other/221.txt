WASHINGTON—A judge on Wednesday postponed a jury trial for onetime Proud Boys members facing seditious conspiracy charges, citing the need for prosecutors and defense lawyers to review materials that will eventually be released by the House committee investigating the Jan. 6, 2021, attack on the Capitol.
During a court hearing in Washington, U.S. District Judge Timothy Kelly pushed the start of the trial from Aug. 8 to Dec. 12. The trial, one of the most important to emerge from the Capitol breach, is expected to last more than a month.
