Kimberly Hammonds hopscotched through a career with executive posts involving increasing responsibilities for technology at  Ford  Motor Co., Dell Inc.,  Boeing Co.  and  Deutsche Bank AG .
In her mid-30s, breast cancer disrupted what looked like steady progress toward a chief executive appointment. “I felt like somebody was basically laying me on the floor and saying you now need to pay attention to what’s important…not everything’s about the career,” she said in a video interview. She began pondering how she would be remembered.
