Almost everything has fallen in the stock market this year. Consumer-staples stocks are bucking the trend.
Shares of companies selling staples such as beer, chocolate and canned soup have raced past the broader market in 2022. Molson Coors Beverage Co. is up 19% for the year, while Hershey Co. has risen 14% and Campbell Soup has gained 11%. That is compared with the S&P 500, which has fallen 18%.
