A livery driver was killed in a hit-and-run crash in the Bronx Sunday morning, cops said.
The 45-year-old man was driving a Toyota Camry west on 168th Street at Webster Avenue in Morrisania when his vehicle was struck by a Chevy around 4:30 a.m., according to police.
The Toyota driver suffered neck and body trauma and died, police said.
The other driver fled the scene on foot.
The Toyota, which had Taxi and Limousine Commission plates, was crushed in the crash, photos show.
