A 54-year-old man was stabbed to death inside a Co-op City apartment Saturday morning, police said.
Police responded to 120 Casals Place at 11:35 a.m. and found the man with a stab wound to his chest.
He was identified by the NYPD as James Santana of 2200 East Tremont Ave., the Bronx.
Santana was taken to Jacobi Hospital where he was pronounced dead.
Police said the victim and attacker knew one another and they were seeking a person of interest in the stabbing.
