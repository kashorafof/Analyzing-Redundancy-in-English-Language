A gunmen opened fire at Pulse nightclub, a popular gay nightclub in Orlando, Fla., killing 49 people and injuring 53 others in the deadliest mass shooting in U.S. history. Gunman Omar Mateen, 29, who was shot and killed by police, called 911 and pledged his allegiance to ISIS after the attack on June 12, 2016. (Reuters/Facebook/Gofundme)
 People from all over the world were identified as the victims of the Orlando, Fla. mass shooting at Pulse nightclub that claimed the lives of 49 people and injured 53 others on June 12, 2016.
